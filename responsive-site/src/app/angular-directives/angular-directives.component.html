In Angular, directives are classes that add additional behavior to elements in your Angular applications. You can attach them to elements in the DOM and manipulate the DOM and its behavior using these directives. There are three main types of directives in Angular:

  1. Component Directives
  2. Structural Directives
  3. Attribute Directives

1. Component Directives Component directives are the most common type of directives in Angular. They are basically Angular components, which have a template, styles, and logic. Each component is a directive with a template.
